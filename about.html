My Story

It all started in August 2015, I finished my course as a Renewable Energy Engineer. At that moment I knew little about programming, I had learned C language during the Engineering course, and there was always a huge desire to learn more because I loved that whole concept "to see it happen" in the middle of "if and else and while's" it was a fantastic experience . But this was not my area of specialization and I worked until September 2021 in the field of engineering projects on energy efficiency and in a last phase in telecommunications at the multinational Visabeira.

Why I change my carrear?

With the coronavirus pandemic and the obligation to work from home, I had free time to dedicate to some new hobbies. I started to study Python and then HTML, CSS and JavaScript and once again I felt in love with the concept of programming and after some time I decided to dedicate all my time to become a front-end developer, I quit my job and joined SheCodes Workshops.
